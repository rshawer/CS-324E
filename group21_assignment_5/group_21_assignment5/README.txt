The first part of the project are the planets that rotate around the sun. They orbit and rotate.
The second part is the large planet Saturn. Saturn spins on its axis, and also has two rings that FLIPS over/wobbles up and down. 
The third part are the comets and asteroids that bounce around the canvas as they rotate around the Y axis.
